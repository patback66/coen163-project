Services

Cruizer's Bar and Grill can be used for events at no cost to anyone who would like to host an event.

 

The patio is a great place to use in the summer and fall for get togethers with family or friends for parties.

 

Our parking lot is great for car shows and poker runs or whatever your imagination can come up with. Most events are done to raise money for good causes like Hospice, Make A Wish Foundation and other charity organizations to raise money for hospital bills, and the military.

 

The inside is great for receptions, engagment and birthday parties, and holiday company parties.

 

Please contact Cruizers at 526-4800 to set up an event.

 

All events get the full support of Cruizers,and the bar and kitchen are there for you to enjoy. Please call ahead with large food orders so the food can be nice and warm for your party.
